## 1. Executive Summary

Hardin AI Solutions is a UK-registered technology company (Hardin Enterprises Ltd, Co. No. 17149514) headquartered in London, England. The business designs and delivers custom AI software, automation systems, SEO strategies and IT modernisation services for UK Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs).

The company was founded in 2024 by Burhan Yanbolu, who identified a clear gap in the market: the majority of UK SMEs are running on outdated software, have poor online visibility, and lack access to affordable AI-powered tools that larger corporations take for granted.

Hardin AI Solutions bridges that gap — delivering enterprise-grade technology at SME-accessible prices.

We are applying for a £25,000 Start Up Loan to fund equipment, office infrastructure, marketing and working capital to accelerate growth from our current early-revenue stage to a sustainable, scalable business.

## 3. The Market Opportunity

- There are **5.5 million SMEs** in the UK, accounting for 99.9% of all businesses
- Only **29% of UK SMEs** have adopted any form of AI or automation (McKinsey, 2024)
- The UK AI market is projected to reach **£803 billion** by 2035 (PwC)
- SMEs that invest in digital tools grow **2.5x faster** than those that don't (Deloitte)
- The demand for affordable, accessible AI solutions for small businesses is growing rapidly

Hardin AI Solutions is positioned at the intersection of this demand — offering bespoke, affordable solutions that larger agencies price out of reach for SMEs.
