How Much Does an
AI Chatbot Cost
for a UK Small Business?

By Hardin AI Solutions
Published 25 April 2026
Read time 5 minutes
Category AI Pricing

If you've been thinking about adding an AI chatbot to your business website, the first question is almost always the same — "how much is this going to cost me?" It's a fair question, and the honest answer is: it depends. But in this article we'll break down exactly what affects the price, what you get at each level, and how UK small businesses can get started without breaking the bank.

The Three Tiers of AI Chatbot Pricing

AI chatbot solutions in the UK generally fall into three categories. Here's a straightforward breakdown:

Type Monthly Cost Setup Cost Best For
DIY tools (Tidio, Crisp) Free – £50/mo None Very basic FAQ bots
Managed AI chatbot (like Hardin AI) £99 – £249/mo £299 – £499 Small businesses wanting real results
Enterprise custom build £500 – £2,000+/mo £2,000 – £10,000+ Large businesses, complex needs

What Do You Actually Get for Your Money?

The price difference between a free DIY tool and a managed AI chatbot isn't just about features — it's about results. Here's what separates them:

Free / cheap DIY tools give you a basic widget you configure yourself. They can answer simple questions but require significant time to set up, often look generic, and don't learn or improve. You're essentially building it yourself with limited support.

Managed AI chatbots (£99–£249/month) are trained specifically on your business — your services, prices, FAQs, tone of voice. They're set up for you, embedded on your site, and actively maintained. They handle complex conversations, capture leads and integrate with your existing tools like calendars and CRMs.

Enterprise solutions are fully bespoke — custom-built AI systems integrated deeply into your business operations. These are for larger organisations with complex requirements and dedicated IT teams.

For most UK small businesses, a managed AI chatbot in the £99–£249/month range delivers the best return on investment. You get professional results without the enterprise price tag or the DIY headache.

What Affects the Price?

Several factors influence how much your AI chatbot will cost:

Number of conversations — Most plans include a monthly conversation limit. Starter plans typically cover 500 conversations/month, which is plenty for most small businesses starting out.

Channels — A chatbot on your website only costs less than one deployed across your website, WhatsApp Business and email simultaneously.

Integrations — Connecting your chatbot to a CRM like HubSpot, a booking system or your e-commerce platform adds to the setup cost but dramatically increases its value.

Training complexity — A simple FAQ bot for a local plumber costs less to train than a detailed product advisor for an e-commerce store with hundreds of items.

Is It Worth the Investment?

Let's put it in perspective. If your AI chatbot captures just two extra leads per month that convert into customers, and your average customer is worth £200, that's £400 in additional revenue against a £99/month cost. The maths works.

Beyond lead capture, consider the time saved. If you currently spend 2 hours a day answering customer enquiries, an AI chatbot handling 80% of those frees up 10+ hours a week. For a small business owner, that time is worth far more than the monthly subscription.

Most Hardin AI clients see a return on their chatbot investment within the first 4–6 weeks — either through captured leads, time saved, or both.
